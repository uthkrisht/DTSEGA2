PredictionMart

Trade the world's questions. Prices = probabilities. Transparent. Verifiable. Simple.

Mission

Make probabilistic insight accessible to everyone by turning complex future events into simple, tradable contracts.

How it works

  1. Define an event: e.g., “Will GDP growth in India exceed 7% in FY26?”
  2. Trade contracts: Buy Yes or No. Each pays $1 if correct.
  3. Price ↔ Probability: $0.72 ≈ 72% probability.
  4. Settle: When outcomes are verified, winning shares pay out.
